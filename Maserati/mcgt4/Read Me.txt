Instructions

1)Put bmm folder in Grand Theft Auto V\update\x64\dlcpacks

2) With OpenIV go to Grand Theft Auto V\update\update.rpf\common\data or if you have a mods folder go to Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update\update.rpf\common\data
 and extract dlclist.xml anywhere you want open it with notepad,

 add this line <Item>dlcpacks:\mcgt4\</Item> and save it.

3) Put the modified dlclist.xml back to Grand Theft Auto V\update\update.rpf\common\data or Grand Theft Auto V\mods\update\update.rpf\common\data for mods folder users and close OpenIV.

4) Ingame spawn the car with any trainer by the name ( mcgt4 ).
